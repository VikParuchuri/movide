Onefrog helps you tell a meaningful story about yourself, your company or app in a clean, modern, captivating one page website.

The feature header lets you use short, to the point statements to quickly give a powerful overview about you, company or app. From these you can drill down to more in depth information.

Amaze people and then tell them a story, by the time they get to the contact section at the bottom they’ll be itching to talk to you.

Feautres

  • Feature navigation header, tell a story that matters.
  • Responsive one page template
  • Google webfont Asap.
  • Browser component for displaying screenshots done in pure CSS. Stand out from the sea of Mac’s and iMac’s used for this purpose.
  • Custom icons designed specifically for Onefrog, clean and easy to understand. Adoble Illustrator (.ai) resource package included.
