More about us

We believe that learning is at times a personal journey, and at others a collaborative experience. An expert teacher and a strong community of peers can completely change the way someone learns. We enable students and teachers to experience the best of self-paced, skill-based learning and community interaction. We want teachers to be able to easily facilitate learning and discussion. We want students to be able to figure out exactly what they have learned, and to help their peers achieve as much as they can. Most of all, we want Movide to be a joy to work with.
