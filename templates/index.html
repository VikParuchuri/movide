More about us

We believe that learning is a personal journey, but we also believe that it can be greatly enhanced by an expert teacher and a strong community of peers. We enable students and teachers to experience the best of self-paced, skill-based learning and community interaction. We want teachers to be able to easily facilitate learning and discussion. We want students to be able to figure out exactly what they have learned, and to help their peers achieve as much as they can. Most of all, we want Movide to be a joy to work with.
